# RDS Northeast SMS Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** February 2025

## Introduction

RDS Northeast ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect mobile phone numbers for our SMS messaging program.

## What Information We Collect

We collect mobile phone numbers from employees and contractors who are part of our on-call support rotation team.

## How We Use Your Information

Mobile phone numbers are used **solely** for sending operational SMS alerts related to job responsibilities, including:
- After-hours support alerts
- On-call notifications for store support requests
- Instructions to respond to or contact support as needed

**We do not use your phone number for marketing purposes.**

## Message Frequency

Message frequency varies. Messages are sent only when after-hours support events occur that require your attention.

## Message and Data Rates

Standard message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile phone service plan.

## Information Sharing

Phone numbers are **not shared with third parties** except as required to deliver messaging services through our SMS provider (Twilio).

## Opt-Out

You may opt out of SMS messages at any time by replying **STOP** to any message. Upon opt-out, you will receive a confirmation message and will not receive any further messages from this campaign.

## Help

For assistance, reply **HELP** to any message, or contact us at the email below.

## Data Retention

Consent records are retained internally for compliance documentation purposes.

## Contact Us

For questions about this Privacy Policy or our SMS program, contact:

**RDS Northeast**  
Email: support@rdsnortheast.com
